Why True Test Type? Unlocking Authentic Knowledge.

In today's digital age, readily available spellcheck and predictive text can inadvertently mask a student's true understanding of spelling and grammar. TrueTest Type creates an environment that mirrors traditional, pen-and-paper exams, ensuring that assessments genuinely reflect a student's core knowledge and writing abilities, with no spellcheck or other digital assistance. This helps educators gain a more accurate insight into learning gaps and provides students with an authentic measure of their skills, preparing them for real-world scenarios where precision matters.

For Students:

  • Show What You Truly Know: Demonstrate your genuine understanding without spellcheck or other digital assistance, building confidence in your unassisted skills.
  • Focus on Content: A clean, distraction-free workspace helps you concentrate solely on expressing your knowledge.
  • Work Safely: Your progress is saved locally for 205 minutes (3 hours and 25 minutes), so you can resume work even if your device closes or loses power.

For Educators:

  • Authentic Assessment: Gain a clearer, more accurate picture of students' spelling, grammar, and overall writing proficiency, free from the influence of spellcheck.
  • Simplified Exam Conditions: Easily implement true test conditions online, disabling spellcheck without complex software installations or system configurations.
  • Boost Exam Integrity: Ensure a fair and consistent testing environment where all students are evaluated on their core abilities, under genuine no-spellcheck conditions.

How It Works: Simple & Secure

TrueTest Type is designed for immediate use. Just load the website, and you're ready to start typing in an environment without spellcheck. Your work is automatically saved locally in your browser, protecting against accidental loss. When you're finished, easily print or save your document as a PDF or text file. It's the quick, easy, and reliable solution for any online exam requiring genuine, unassisted writing.
